Big Cat Tournament

Think you’ve got what it takes to reel in the biggest catch? Join us this weekend for an unforgettable Big Catfish Tournament! With up to 10 hours of fishing, cash prizes, and plenty of fun, this is your chance to show off your skills and take home the glory. Whether you’re in it for the competition or just a good time by the water, we’ve got something for everyone. Don’t miss out—register today and get ready to cast your line for the big one!

  • When: Every Weekend (Friday - Sunday)
  • Time: 4:00 PM - 2:00 AM
  • Buy-In: $50
  • Entry Fee: $25
  • Prizes: 75% payout for first place, 25% for the second largest.
