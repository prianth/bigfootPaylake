About Bigfoot Paylake

Located in the heart of Eastern Kentucky, Bigfoot Paylake is your go-to destination for fishing and relaxation. Whether you're here for sport or leisure, we have something for everyone.

The 2025 fishing season will officially begin on April 17th. Our operating hours will start at 3:00 PM on Thursdays and conclude at 6:00 PM on Sundays. We look forward to seeing you!

Think you’ve got what it takes to reel in the biggest catch? Join us this weekend for an unforgettable Big Catfish Tournament!
